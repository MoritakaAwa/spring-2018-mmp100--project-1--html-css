羽生結弦 Yuzuru Hanyu

Yuzuru Hanyu is a Japanese pro figure skater from Miyazaki Japan. He is 23 years old and was born in 1994 December 7th. He is a two-time Olympic champion (2014, 2018), a two-time World champion (2014, 2017), a four-time Grand Prix Final champion (2013–2016), a three-time Four Continents silver medalist (2011, 2013, 2017), the 2010 World Junior champion, the 2009–10 Junior Grand Prix Final champion, and a four-time Japanese national champion (2012–2015). He has also medaled at three other World Championships, taking bronze in 2012, and silver in 2015 and 2016. Hanyu has broken world records 12 times and currently holds the world records for the hightest short program, free skating, and combined total scores. He's defenitely one of the greatest figur skater in history.

Hanyu apparently loves the Disney character Pooh bear and his supporters always throw the adorable bears onto the ice after he perform. There’s usually a Pooh sitting rinkside when he skates, and Hanyu will bow to or pat the bear’s head before competin. He sees Pooh as a good luck mascot.
